# Journey Pipeline

## Purpose

Transform raw travel materials into a published Journey page while preserving the author's voice. Preserve the author's narrative voice. Never replace a vivid scene with a structural heading. Structure should guide the writing, not become visible to the reader.

---

## Inputs

- garden/capture/<place>.md
- Photos (optional)

---

## Workflow

Step 1

Read the capture file.

Do not rewrite immediately.

Identify

- timeline
- important scenes
- turning points
- recurring themes

---

Step 2

Produce a curated Journey Note.

Requirements

- preserve my voice
- remove repetition
- do not invent facts
- keep chronology
- highlight insights

Save as

garden/notes/<place>.md

---

Step 3

Review manually.

Add reflections that only I can write.

---

Step 4

Generate

journeys/<place>.qmd

using

journeys/journey-template.qmd

---

Step 5

Link images.

Export only web-sized images.

---

Output

A published Journey page.
